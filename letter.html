A Letter for You

This letter contains the deepest words I could never say out loud. I hope it reaches you with the warmth and sincerity I intend. Thank you for being in my life.

I may not always find the right words, but know that my love for you is constant and real. You bring light even in my darkest days. You make me laugh a little louder, smile a little bigger, and live a little better. I’m beyond grateful to walk this life with you.

With every beat of this song, may you feel how much I cherish you. Always remember, no matter what happens or where life takes us— it’s always been you, and it will always be you.
