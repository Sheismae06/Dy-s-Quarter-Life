This moment was when we went to MOA. That day also, we had a misunderstanding but honestly, it’s also the day I truly felt how much you love me. Even when I kept pushing you away and tried to break up with you, you never left. Tinakbuhan kita and tinaguan, hindi ka napagod hanapin ako. Sana soon, makabalik tayo ulit doon... pero this time, kasama na natin ang little ones natin if papalarin at ibibigay na ni Lord.

I love this picture too, kasi ito yung time na nag-bike tayo sa Riverbanks after natin lakarin yung PAG-IBIG mo. Ang init noon, pero sobrang saya kasi we were just enjoying our time. Naalala mo 'to? Ito yung time na umalis tayo tapos nakalimutan natin magdala ng payong. That day reminded me of our first time na naligo tayo sa ulan, way back 2019. Para tayong mga bata—takbuhan, tawanan, at enjoy lang sa ulan. Tinulungan mo akong kalimutan 'yung sakit at matutunang mahalin ang ulan. Thank you, love.

I have a lot of favorite pictures—actually, lahat ng photos natin together are my favorites. Minsan lang rin tayo nakakakuha ng pictures kasi we’re always so caught up enjoying our moments together. And for me, that’s enough. I can’t wait to capture more memories with you and build a big happy family together.
